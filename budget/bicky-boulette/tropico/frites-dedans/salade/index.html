The Golden Triangle

Geography plays a massive role in the search for the perfect durum. Our data shows a distinct concentration of high-quality establishments in specific municipalities.

Schaerbeek emerges as the undisputed heartland of authentic Turkish kebabs in Brussels. The "Chaussée de Haecht" is often referred to as the Kebab mile. Meanwhile, the Centre caters to the late-night crowd and tourists, offering reliability and accessibility.

Deconstructing the Legend

What actually goes into a Brussels Durum? It's not just meat in bread. It is a carefully stacked architecture of flavor.

Vertical Stack Analysis
The Wrap (Pain Durum)
Lightly toasted, ideally fresh dough (Lavash)
The Sauce (Crucial)
Andalouse, Samouraï, or Garlic. Applied liberally.
The Protein (Meat)
Layered Veal & Turkey or Chicken. Sliced thin.
The Crunch (Crudités)
Lettuce, Tomatoes, Onions (STO), sometimes Red Cabbage.
The Belgian Touch (Fries)
Placed INSIDE the wrap. Must be crispy.
