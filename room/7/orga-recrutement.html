The Carbon Reality

While Google is carbon neutral for operations today, the goal is Net Zero across the entire value chain. The data reveals a significant challenge: as infrastructure grows to support AI, total emissions have risen, driven primarily by "Scope 3" (supply chain) emissions.

Key Insight:

Scope 3 (Value Chain) accounts for over 75% of the total footprint, primarily from hardware manufacturing and data center construction.

Energy & Water Stewardship

Sustainable computing isn't just about electricity; it's about water for cooling and the timing of energy usage. Google is pioneering the "24/7 Carbon-Free Energy" approach and investing heavily in watershed health.
